The Grand Budapest Hotel (2014)

Synopsis

The film stars Ralph Fiennes as Monsieur Gustave H., a legendary concierge of a famous European hotel in the fictional country of Zubrowka.

When Gustave is framed for the murder of a wealthy dowager, he and his recently befriended lobby boy, Zero, embark on a quest for fortune and a priceless Renaissance painting.
