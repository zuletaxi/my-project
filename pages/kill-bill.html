Kill Bill: Vol. 1 (2003)

Synopsis

Directed by Quentin Tarantino, the film follows a character known as "The Bride," a former assassin who wakes up from a four-year coma.

She embarks on a mission to seek revenge on the team of assassins who betrayed her and their leader, Bill.
